Name
Season Mirror

Description
Creates 1 Greater Fairy when built, this building will also generate 1 each week and 1 Lesser Fairy each day. Enemy Lesser Fairies will also spawn on neutral battles if your hero isn't a fairy hero, other factions are also affected by this. The types of fairy depends on season (on Autumn, 2 Zombie Fairies will be created instead of Greater Fairies).
