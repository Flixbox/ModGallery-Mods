LORE MAIN RACE:
Antfolk

LORE HISTORY:
All kinds of alate ants have gathered deep underground to build one massive hidden nest, forming and alliance, learning communication and socialization between species. After some generations, thanks to evolutionary magic, some have managed to evolve into big & intelligent antfolk. Using their newfound intelligence they will lead Colony in the revolution against the overground.

LORE CULTURE:
Antfolk are intelligent beings, learning by observing the races in the overground, their nest is interconnected across the underground, safe from any sudden attacks. Some have learned to control magic and the smartest formed a council made of Monarchs & Scholars that lead the antfolk on their queens' behalf. They also help the adult ants evolve once they gather enough knowledge with the use of evolutionary magic.

LORE HERO FIGHTER:
In charge of economy and military. The Monarchs are highly skilled leaders and tacticians, using only their antennae to issue commands to their legions.

LORE HERO CASTER:
Known as the smartest and most skilled in magic antfolk, Scholars are in charge of teaching new generations of antfolk the way of evolution and elemental magic.
