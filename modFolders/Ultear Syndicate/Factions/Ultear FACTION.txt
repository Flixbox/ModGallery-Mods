LORE HISTORY:
The Ultear Syndicate are an off world society that aims to harvest resources to supply their ever expanding society. Led by massive mega corporations, nearly every member of Ultearan society is on a contract of some sort. They are always looking to add new species to their empire.

LORE CULTURE:
Most Ultearan citizens value their comfortable lives greatly and consider any means to gather wealth, with the intention to fund their extravigant lifestyles, some citizens turn to mercenary work but the military also pays well.

LORE HERO FIGHTER:
Higher ranking individuals within the Ultearan military culture, the Military Directors lead military operations on the frontier.

LORE HERO CASTER:
Soldiers of Fortune are pricey mercenaries and military leaders on contract with the corporations that run Ultearan Society.
